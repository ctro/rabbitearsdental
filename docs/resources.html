Scheduling

Your dental needs are unique, and we will schedule you for those exact needs. Please call Rabbit Ears Dental at 970-879-5630 to schedule your appointment today!

Instructions

Before Treatment

Please continue to take all medications as prescribed by your physician, unless stated otherwise by your dentist. Be sure to eat a normal meal before your appointment. As instructed by your physician, take any prescribed dental antibiotic prophylaxis for mitral valve prolapse, heart murmur, joint prosthesis, cardiac prosthesis, or rheumatic heart disease.

After Treatment

Please follow closely the postoperative instructions given to you by your doctor, hygienist, and dental assistant.

Location

We are conveniently located at the edge of downtown Steamboat Springs at the corner of South Lincoln Ave and Hilltop Lane.

Payments

Payment is due at the time service is rendered, unless previous arrangements have been made.

If you have dental insurance, your estimated portion is due at the time service is rendered, unless previous arrangements have been made.

Payment plans are available only with a credit card. Your balance can be split into 3 equal monthly payments with an additional 25$ service fee.
